About

The Titcoin Community is an online community of pornography/erotica consumers and creators. We have our own cryptocurrency, called Titcoin, which we use to tip each other for uploading content! Our goal is having FUN, first and foremostly, and secondly making it profitable for everyone to hold some titcoins.

If you are an adult content creator and would like to join our community, the only thing you have to do is to mention #titcoin in your content and post a link to it in one of the existing forums or chats! In order to receive titcoins, take a look at the Using Titcoin page.

History

The Titcoin cryptocurrency was created in early 2014 by the entrepreneurs Edward Mansfield and Richard Allen as "the official digital currency of the adult entertainment industry". It was among of the first 20 altcoins (Wikipedia) and received broad attention by mainstream media in 2014. Titcoin received two nominations as the "Alternative Payment Services Company of the Year" at the XBIZ Awards ceremony in 2015 and 2016.

Titcoin did not attract much interest in the following years from 2016 to 2018 and development halted. The official project accounts were first sold to the adult game development studio Joy-Toilet, which finally sold them to TittieCoin, a cryptocurrency rival of Titcoin. In September 2018, Titcoin community member Gandalf86 took over development by updating the Titcoin wallet to an up-to-date Bitcoin codebase under his own accounts. (Announcement)
