Download

This is the official Titcoin wallet, created by Gandalf86. You can use it to send and receive titcoins and securely store them on your own PC. No registration is necessary. For a comparison of the different usage options, see Using Titcoin!

Windows (64 bit): titcoin-0.16.3.2-win64-setup.exe (13.6 MB)
Mac (64 bit): Titcoin-0.16.3.2.dmg (33.8 MB)
Linux (Source code): titcoin-0.16.3-titcoin2.tar.gz (5.4 MB)

Unfortunately we do not have a mobile app yet. We will try to make this happen as soon as possible! In the meantime, please try using the above software on your PC and tell us how you like it. For help with installing and using Titcoin on your PC, please take a look at the Screenshots page or visit us on Discord.
