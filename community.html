Community

This is a list of our current community channels, but you are free to create your own ones and contact us to add them here!

  • ShowMyTitcoin (Reddit): Our main community platform! Girls can post pictures/videos of themselves and get tipped with Titcoin.
  • ShowMyTitCoin community chat: Currently our main chat room.
  • Mastodon: Social media channel to post project news and share community content.
  • Telegram: Community-operated Telegram group
  • Bitcointalk: Forum for cryptocurrency enthusiasts (ANN thread)

The following channels are unmaintained, inactive or otherwise defunct:

  • Discord: Previously our main chat room, has been abandoned in favor of the ShowMyTitCoin community chat.
  • Titcoinity, CryptoTitCoin: Old Reddit communities that never received much attention.
  • The Daily Titcoin: Twitter account that is currently inactive
  • OfficialTitcoin: Previously official Titcoin account on Twitter, has been bought by TittieCoin (see the FAQ)
